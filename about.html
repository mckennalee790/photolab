About Me

My name is McKenna Lee Hansen, and I looooooove photography! I grew up in Ogden, Utah surrounded by the beautiful mountains. These mountains became my inspiration for photography and capturing the beauty that nature has to offer.

My husband and I live in American Fork, Utah, where we enjoy incredible views. We love watching movies and going on bike rides together. My passions include running, reading, and of course, photography.

I began my career as a photographer in 2023. I started with family portraits and wedding photography, but then found that my passion was in taking pictures of nature.

Go check out my portfolio to see some of my favorite shots!
